About Us

About Amacec Company Limited

Amacec Kenya Ltd is a privately-owned Kenyan company licensed by:

  • Energy and Petroleum Regulatory Authority (EPRA)
  • Communications Authority of Kenya (CAK)
  • and registered by National Construction Authority (NCA) under Electrical Engineering classes.

We are fully recognised in the Oil & Gas, Energy sectors, Commercial Buildings, Education, Health care & Hospitality, NGOs industries .

We have been providing services in Electrical Power Systems, Data & voice cabling, Instrumentations, Control systems, Fiber Optics Data center cabling.

Our core mandate is to offer smart solutions and system integration by providing services from design, supply, installation, testing and commissioning. Additionally, we offer after sales service and maintenance.

Vision

To be a reliable and dependable Electrical Engineering Company offering solutions in the region and beyond.

Mission

To build lasting relationship with our customers by providing impeccable Electrical Engineering services.

Regulation and Compliance

The Company is duly registered and compliant with:

  1. National Construction Authority (NCA) - NCA1
  2. Energy and Petroleum Regulatory Authority (EPRA) - A1
  3. Communication Authority of Kenya (CAK) - CLASS A
  4. Information Communication and Technology Authority (ICTA) - ICT4

  • Our technical approach on customer requirement

We work closely with the customer to understand their requirements and/or needs then developer a complete engineering draft for the services required.

This is by using the existing drawings of the old system or by carrying out site surveys for new requirements gathering.

Our mandate at Amacec is to come up with complete design for new or the required upgrade for the existing switchgears, drives for both medium and low voltage, telecommunication services, and control systems.

Key Services

At Amacec Company Limited, we offer a wide range of smart solutions.

Instrumentation & Control Systems

Supply, installation, testing and commissioning of measurements equipment for liquid, pressure and flow.

Supply, configuration, testing and commissioning of Programmable Logic Control (PLC), SCADA, Human Interphase (HMI) and instrumentation devices.

Process Automation (System Integration)

To understand the heart of communication protocols between two different Systems, Amacec has developed human capital to ensure there is smooth running of operation whenever there are two or more different systems installed in an organization to communicate with each other.

Electrical Works

Design, supply, installation, testing and commissioning of High Volage step down Transformers, (Medium Voltage, Low Voltage, Drives) Switchgears & Automation.

Telecommunication Services

Installation, upgrading as well as maintenance of Network infrastructure for Data & Voice, Wireless LAN/WAN, Provision of internet services, Fibre Optics for backbone and campus, CCTVs etc
